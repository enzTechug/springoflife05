Our Core Programs

Comprehensive care designed to meet the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of elders in our community.

Home Visits & Care

Weekly visits offering companionship, hygiene support, bedding cleaning, basic care and ensuring safe living conditions for our elders.

Health & Welfare Support

Clinic referrals, wound care follow-up, medication reminders, and urgent case response to ensure proper healthcare access.

Spiritual & Emotional Care

Prayer, encouragement, and counseling that restore hope and community connection through faith-based support.

Nutritional Support

Weekly food packages containing nutritious meals and essential supplies to combat malnutrition and ensure well-being.

Community Gatherings

Monthly gatherings where elders share warm meals, stories, and build meaningful connections to combat loneliness.

Emergency Response

Immediate assistance for high-risk elders including emergency medical care, shelter repairs, and crisis intervention.

BE PART OF THE CHANGE

Every small act of kindness restores dignity to an elderly person. You can help today through prayer, volunteering, or a monthly gift.
