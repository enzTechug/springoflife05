Our Story

Spring of Life began with a simple calling: to bring dignity, care, and companionship to elderly men and women who had been forgotten. After witnessing the daily struggles of vulnerable elders in Zombo District—loneliness, hunger, and little access to medical support—we felt moved to act.

What started as a small personal effort has grown into a community initiative that provides food support, home visits, health assistance, and opportunities for elders to stay active through traditional Alur handcrafts. Spring of Life continues to serve with compassion, transparency, and the belief that every elderly person deserves to be seen, valued, and cared for.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to support vulnerable elderly people in Zombo District with food, home visits, healthcare assistance, and meaningful community engagement—restoring dignity, hope, and love to their lives.

Natural Products With Purpose
Spring of Life Naturals creates simple, safe, and effective skincare and wellness products using locally sourced Ugandan ingredients. Every purchase supports our elderly programs.

Our Products Include:

  • Avocado-based soaps and creams
